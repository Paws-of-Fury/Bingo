How It Works

What is Clan Bingo?

A 15-day team competition running March 27 – April 10. Each day, new tasks are revealed. Your team completes the task, submits proof, and earns points. The team with the most points at the end wins!

This bingo is open to everyone so feel free to invite your friends!

You will have a 4 hour window that your team decides and you can only submit during that window.

Buy Ins

  • Clan members: 10M
  • Non clan members: 50M

Contact @Saltea or @Aevla (Vitalis Owner Vla) to buy in.

Tasks

New tasks are revealed every day. Times may vary based on availability.

Tasks have different point values based on difficulty:

Bronze 1–2 points (easier tasks)
Silver 3–5 points (medium tasks)
Gold 6+ points (harder tasks)

You can catch up on missed days — submit for any past task your team hasn't done yet.

How to Submit

Your team has a daily submission window. You can only submit during your window.

You will be given a phrase for submissions, this is different to your passphrase. You will need to put this in your notes and/or type it in your chatbox for submissions to count.

On Discord

  1. Use /submit in the Discord server
  2. Pick which task you're submitting for from the dropdown
  3. Attach up to 4 screenshots as proof
  4. An admin will review and approve or deny your submission
  5. If approved, your team gets the points!

On the Website

  1. Click Login and enter your team's passphrase
  2. Click on a day's task and hit Submit Proof
  3. Upload your screenshots and submit

Rules

  • You must submit during your team's time slot
  • Submissions need to be approved by an admin before points count
  • All team members can submit on behalf of the team
  • You will be given a phrase for submissions, this is different to your passphrase. You will need to put this in your notes and/or type it in your chatbox for submissions to count.
  • You can only play one account to submit with.

Leaderboard

  • Use /bleaderboard on Discord to see team standings
  • The website leaderboard also shows live standings

Website

Check the bingo website to:

  • See all revealed tasks on the day grid
  • View task details and point values
  • Check the leaderboard
  • Submit proof (when logged in)

How Teams Work

Teams are based on region and/or friendship. We also try to balance based on skill level.

You can request up to one person to be on the same team. Use /brequest theirname in Discord. You must both request each other.
